Featured Case Study

How we scaled a barbershop
to 10,000+ bookings/day

The Problem

Minore Barbershop was managing appointments over the phone across two locations. Staff were overwhelmed, double-bookings were common, and there was no visibility into daily revenue or barber performance.

The Solution

We built a complete appointment platform with QR code access, real-time dashboards, automated emails, and a CI/CD pipeline. Deployed on Render with PostgreSQL and Sentry monitoring.
